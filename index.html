Our Story of Excellence

Founded in 2020, Avenue Nails & Spa began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where guests could escape the demands of everyday life and reconnect with their inner selves.

Over the years, we have refined our approach to wellness, combining time-honored traditions with innovative techniques. Our team of expert therapists brings decades of experience and a passion for healing to every treatment.

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized care. We believe that each guest is unique, with individual needs and preferences. That's why we take the time to understand your wellness goals and tailor our services accordingly.

Emma Thompson

Founder & Lead Therapist
